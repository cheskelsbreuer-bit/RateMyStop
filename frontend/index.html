For Agencies

Are you the agency people are writing about? Sign in to post official responses, see your dashboard, and reach the public directly.

We verify by email domain. Agency emails like @villagespringvalley.org, @fdny.nyc.gov, @dmv.ny.gov, @nyc.gov are auto-verified.

What you can do once verified

  • Reply to any story about your agency with a ✅ Verified [Your Agency] badge
  • Subscribe to alerts when new stories mention your agency
  • Access a sentiment dashboard (replies, recognitions, concerns) — coming with the paid tier
